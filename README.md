# Expo Exchange (萬博會)

**Expo Exchange** is a decentralized "Exotic Derivatives" protocol designed to tokenize and trade the world's most critical data fluctuations. 

### 🌟 Why "Expo"?
Inspired by the "World Expo," our platform is a grand exhibition of diverse trading pools. We move beyond simple token price speculation, allowing users to hedge or speculate on the underlying forces of the digital and physical economy.

### 🚀 Featured Pools
- **Network Infrastructure:** Gas Price Perp, Hashrate Perp.
- **Economic Indicators:** Stablecoin De-peg Index, LSD Yield Rates.
- **Social Momentum:** Meme Sentiment Index, Trending Topic Volatility.
